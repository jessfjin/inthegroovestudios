Classes & Workshops

At In The Groove Studios, we offer a variety of world-class hip hop classes and more.

Adult Classes

Hip Hop, K-Pop, Dancehall, Urban Contemporary, House, Bodywerk... All of our classes are geared for the adults unless otherwise specified.

Youth Classes

Youth classes are available for those ages 7 to 17. Classes will be labeled as such on the schedule.

Specialty Classes

We also offer a fantastic selection of Afro Dance, Cuban style Salsa and Rueda, Jazz Funk, Samba, and more!
